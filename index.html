Frequently asked

Questions we get a lot.

What does an AI automation actually do?

It takes a task you currently do by hand — sorting emails, building weekly reports, tracking expenses, chasing follow-ups — and runs it for you on a schedule or in response to a trigger. You stay in the loop without doing the busywork.

Do I need to be technical to work with you?

No. We handle the build and the setup. You describe the outcome you want; we figure out how to get there. Most of our clients have never written a line of code.

How long does setup take?

Most single-workflow setups go live within 1–2 weeks of the discovery call. Larger automation stacks (multiple workflows) typically take 3–4 weeks.

What tools do you work with?

The ones you already use. Gmail, Google Workspace, Slack, Notion, QuickBooks, Stripe, Shopify, Airtable, Zapier, Make, Cloudflare, and dozens more. We meet your stack where it is.

What if my needs change?

We expect them to. Every package includes monthly check-ins where we adjust, optimize, or replace workflows as your business evolves.

Do you only build automations, or can you build apps too?

We also ship native apps under our partnership program — that's how we built TinkerTaps and Trivd. See partner.courtrightco.com for that side of the studio.

Where are you based?

Nashville, Tennessee. We work with clients across the United States remotely.
