06 — Frequently Asked

Questions we get a lot.

What does an AI automation actually do?

It takes a task you currently do by hand. Sorting emails, building reports, tracking expenses, chasing follow-ups. And runs it for you on a schedule or in response to a trigger. You stay in the loop without doing the busywork.

Do I need to be technical?

No. We handle the build and the setup. You describe the outcome; we figure out how to get there. Most clients have never written a line of code.

How long does setup take?

Most single-workflow setups go live within 1-2 weeks. Larger stacks of multiple workflows take 3-4 weeks.

What tools do you work with?

The ones you already use. Gmail, Google Workspace, Slack, Notion, QuickBooks, Stripe, Shopify, Airtable, Zapier, Make, Cloudflare, and dozens more. We meet your stack where it is.

What if my needs change?

We expect them to. Every package includes monthly check-ins where we adjust, optimize, or replace workflows as your business evolves.

Do you build apps too?

Yes. Native apps run under our partnership program at partner.courtrightco.com. Simple websites and landing pages are included right here in our automation work.
