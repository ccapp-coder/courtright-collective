SMS Opt-In & Consent Flow

Public, screenshot-backed documentation of how Aimtogro collects SMS consent — provided so the opt-in experience can be verified without an account. Operated by Courtright Collective LLC, Nashville, TN. The live app is at aimtogro.com.

Overview

Aimtogro is a business-management app for small-business owners. Our SMS program sends messages only to the account owner (about their own account), and to callers who contact the owner's business line. There are two independent opt-in workflows, both documented below with screenshots of the actual product. We never send marketing or promotional texts through this program, and we never share mobile numbers with third parties for marketing.

Workflow 1 — Account owner opt-in (account notifications)

The business owner opts in inside the app, at Settings → Notifications. Because this screen is part of the signed-in product, the screenshots below show exactly what the owner sees so the consent experience is publicly verifiable.

  1. The owner signs in at aimtogro.com and opens Settings → Notifications.
  2. They enter their own mobile number in the “Your phone” field. The consent disclosure is shown directly beneath the field.
  3. They click “Send code,” receive a one-time code by SMS, and enter it to verify their number. This active verification is the consent — nothing is pre-enabled, and notifications stay disabled until a phone is verified.
  4. They choose which alerts to receive (overdue invoices, stale quotes, voicemail leads, etc.).
The live opt-in screen: aimtogro.com/dashboard/settings/notifications. The owner enters their number, reads the disclosure, and verifies with a one-time code.

The exact consent language shown on that screen, directly beneath the phone field, reads:

“By verifying your number you agree to receive account alerts, reminders, and notifications you select via SMS from Aimtogro. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. See our SMS Terms and Privacy Policy.”

Workflow 2 — Inbound missed-call text-back

When a person voluntarily calls the owner's connected business line and the call goes unanswered, Aimtogro sends that caller a single automated text-back identifying the business and inviting them to reply. Consent is the caller's own act of initiating contact with the business. The caller is not added to any list, and receives no marketing or recurring messages. This feature is managed under the owner's Secretary workspace:

The owner's Secretary workspace, where the missed-call auto-text-back and voicemail features are managed.

Program terms

At a glance

  • Program name: Aimtogro account notifications & missed-call replies
  • Message frequency: varies based on account activity and the alerts the owner selects
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop all messages
  • Help: reply HELP, or email support@courtrightco.com
  • Privacy: mobile numbers and consent are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing — see the Privacy Policy
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages
