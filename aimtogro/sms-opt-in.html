SMS Program & Opt-In

How our text messages work and how you consent · Operated by Courtright Collective LLC

What the Aimtogro SMS program is

Aimtogro is a business-management app for small-business owners. Our SMS program sends text messages only to the account owner, and only about their own business account. We send two kinds of messages:

  • Account notifications — alerts about the owner's own account activity, such as overdue invoices, stale quotes, and daily or weekly business summaries.
  • Missed-call text-back replies — when a caller phones the owner's connected business line and the call goes unanswered, Aimtogro sends that caller one automated text inviting them to reply so the business can follow up.

We never send marketing or promotional texts through this program, and we never share mobile numbers with third parties for their own marketing.

How you opt in

There are two consent paths, matching the two message types above.

1. Account notifications (owner opt-in)

During signup, and any time afterward in Settings → Notifications inside the Aimtogro web app at aimtogro.courtrightco.com, the business owner enters their own mobile number and checks an unchecked consent box. Consent is never pre-checked and is never required to use the app. The opt-in screen looks like this:

2. Missed-call text-back (caller-initiated)

When someone calls a business owner's connected business line and the call is not answered, Aimtogro sends that caller a single automated text inviting them to reply. Consent is established by the caller voluntarily initiating contact with the business. The first message identifies the business, explains why they're receiving it, and includes opt-out instructions. We do not add these callers to any notification or marketing list.

Program terms

At a glance

  • Program name: Aimtogro account notifications & missed-call replies
  • Message frequency: varies based on your account activity
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop all messages
  • Help: reply HELP, or email info@courtrightco.com
  • Carriers: carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages

Opting out & getting help

You can stop messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You'll receive one confirmation that you've been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent. To resume, reply START. For help, reply HELP or contact us at info@courtrightco.com.
