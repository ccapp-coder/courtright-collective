Automated business reports & dashboards.

Stop building reports by hand. We pull data from the tools you already use, generate the report on a schedule, and deliver it to your inbox. Formatted, ready to read.

What this typically replaces

If you're spending Monday morning exporting CSVs from Stripe, Shopify, your CRM, your accounting software, and then copy-pasting into a Google Sheet, this is for you. Most owners we work with spend 2-4 hours per week building reports they could be reading in 5 minutes.

What we build

Weekly sales report

Every Monday at 7am: revenue, top products, conversion rate, top channels, week-over-week comparison. Delivered to your inbox, ready to act on.

Monthly business snapshot

Revenue, expenses, customer metrics, growth rate, AR/AP status. The kind of report you'd build for a board meeting, generated automatically.

Live KPI dashboard

A web dashboard showing your most important numbers in real time. Open it on your phone before a meeting, on a TV in the office, on a tab you keep open all day.

Anomaly alerts

The AI watches for unusual patterns. A 30% drop in conversion. A customer who hasn't ordered in 90 days. A vendor invoice way off. And pings you before it becomes a problem.

Custom client reports

If you bill clients with monthly performance reports (agencies, service providers), we build them once and they generate themselves every month. Each client gets their numbers, branded.
