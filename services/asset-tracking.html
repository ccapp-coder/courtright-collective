AI asset & inventory tracking.

Equipment, inventory, contracts, leases, subscriptions — automatically tracked, alerted on, and reported. Nothing falls through the cracks.

What this typically replaces

Most small businesses track assets in a spreadsheet that gets updated when someone remembers. A lease renews unnoticed. A piece of equipment goes out of warranty. A SaaS subscription auto-renews at a higher rate. The cost of "I didn't know" adds up fast.

What we build

Living asset register

Every piece of equipment, every contract, every lease, every recurring subscription — tracked with key dates, costs, renewal terms, and owners. Updated automatically from bank feeds, calendars, and emails.

Renewal & expiration alerts

60 days before a contract auto-renews, you get a heads-up. 30 days before a warranty expires, you get a reminder. The AI watches the calendar so you don't have to.

Inventory reconciliation

If you carry inventory, the AI cross-checks your POS, your shipping system, and your physical counts. Discrepancies get flagged the day they happen, not at quarter-end.

Subscription audit

How much are you spending on SaaS? Which seats are unused? Which subscriptions auto-renewed at higher rates? The AI runs a monthly audit and gives you a "cancel this" list.

Compliance & insurance tracking

Licenses, insurance policies, certifications — tracked with expiration dates and renewal workflows. Never let coverage lapse.
