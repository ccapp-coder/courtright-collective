FAQ

About the pricing.

Why no hourly billing?

Hourly creates the wrong incentive. The faster we build, the less we make. The slower we build, the more we make. We prefer fixed scope and fixed price so we're aligned with you on shipping fast, not stretching things out.

Can I switch tiers as my needs change?

Yes. Move up or down as your business evolves. Annual prepay gets you 10% off any tier.

What if I want to cancel?

Monthly tiers run month-to-month with 30 days notice. Quick Starts are one-time except for the maintenance component, which is the same 30-day cancellation. No long contracts, no fine print.

Are the Quick Start prices firm?

Yes for the listed scope. If your situation needs more custom work, we'll quote that separately. The whole point of Quick Starts is you know what you're paying before you book.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes for projects $2,000+, typically 50% upfront and 50% on completion. For longer engagements, we set up monthly billing.

What about app partnerships?

That's a separate offering with revenue share pricing, not monthly fees. See partner.courtrightco.com.
