A family-run studio for the small businesses we'd want to work with.

Courtright Collective is an independent automation and app studio in Nashville, Tennessee. We build AI workflows that take the busywork off small business owners' plates — and occasionally, when something needs to be a product, we ship that too.

What we believe

Most small businesses don't need a full-time AI hire. They need one workflow built well, deployed cleanly, and maintained when their business changes. That's it. That's the whole job. We exist because hiring a developer is overkill, signing up for a SaaS tool is underkill, and consultants charge $400/hour to tell you what you already know.

We're a small team that handles the build and the upkeep. You describe the outcome you want; we make it happen.

How we got here

We started Courtright Collective as an indie app studio — that's how TinkerTaps and Trivd came to be. Building apps is creative, slow work, and the wins are far apart. As AI automation tools matured, we kept hearing from small-business owners who needed exactly the same thing: workflows built for them, by people who actually understood the work.

So we pivoted the main studio to focus on AI automation services. The app side lives on under our partnership program for founders ready to ship.

How we work

We don't hourly-bill. We don't disappear after launch. We don't write proposals so big you need a glossary. Every engagement is scoped clearly, priced flatly, and built with monthly check-ins so the work keeps fitting your business.

We're based in Nashville. We work with clients across the country remotely. If you're nearby and want to meet in person, we'd love that too.
