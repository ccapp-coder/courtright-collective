Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Courtright Collective LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect from visitors and clients of courtrightco.com, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

When you visit the site, we collect minimal usage data via Cloudflare Web Analytics — a privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics service. This includes page views, referrer, country, and device type. We do not collect your IP address in identifiable form, and we do not use tracking cookies.

When you contact us (via email, the booking form, or any other channel), we collect the information you choose to share — typically your name, email address, and the details of your inquiry.

When you become a client, we collect the information needed to deliver the service you've engaged us for. This may include access credentials to specific tools (which we handle through secure password managers and revoke immediately upon engagement end).

How we use it

We use your information solely to deliver the services you've requested, to respond to inquiries, and to send occasional updates if you've opted in. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This site uses Cloudflare for hosting and DNS. Bookings may be handled through Cal.com (when active) which has its own privacy policy. Email correspondence flows through Google Workspace. Each of these has industry-standard privacy controls.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time what information we hold about you, request that we correct it, or request that we delete it. Email hello@courtrightco.com with the request.

Cookies

This site does not set tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set essential cookies for security and performance.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle data, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
